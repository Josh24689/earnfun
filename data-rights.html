Data Subject Rights

Last Updated: July 2026


EarnFun respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains your rights regarding your personal data and how you can exercise those rights.

Your Rights

Depending on the laws that apply to you, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data associated with your EarnFun account.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Request deletion of your EarnFun account and associated personal data where permitted by law.
  • Request restriction of certain processing activities.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information in certain situations.
  • Request a copy of your personal data in a portable format where applicable.

How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us by email using the address below.

Email:
support.earnfun@gmail.com

To help us process your request, please include:

  • Your registered email address.
  • The type of request (access, correction, deletion, etc.).
  • Any additional information that helps us identify your account.

Identity Verification

To protect your account and personal information, we may request additional information to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

Response Time

We aim to respond to legitimate requests within 30 days. If additional time is required, we will inform you and explain the reason for the delay.

Related Policies

Data Subject Rights

At EarnFun, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This page explains your rights regarding the personal information we collect and how you can exercise those rights.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information associated with your EarnFun account.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated personal information.
  • Delete your EarnFun account and associated personal data, where permitted by law.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your personal information.
  • Request a copy of your personal data in a portable format where applicable.

How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email below.

Email: support.earnfun@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Your request (access, correction, deletion, etc.).
  • Any additional information that will help us identify your account.

Verification

To protect your account and personal information, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. If additional time is required, we will notify you.

More Information

For more details about how EarnFun collects, uses, stores, and protects your information, please read our Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026


Welcome to EarnFun. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal information when using the EarnFun mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use EarnFun, we may collect:

  • Email address.
  • Firebase Authentication user ID.
  • Google account information when signing in with Google.
  • Display name and profile photo (if available).
  • Reward points and wallet balance.
  • Referral information.
  • Reward history and withdrawal requests.
  • Support requests submitted through the app.
  • Basic device information required for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate your identity securely.
  • Track rewards and referrals.
  • Process withdrawals.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve the performance and reliability of EarnFun.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or suspicious activity.
  • Send important notifications related to your account.

3. Third-Party Services

EarnFun uses trusted third-party services that may process certain information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • CPX Research
  • Start.io
  • Future advertising and offerwall partners

4. Surveys, Offerwalls & Advertisements

When participating in surveys, offerwalls, or advertisements, additional information may be collected directly by the advertising partner to verify completion, prevent fraud, and determine eligibility for rewards.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of our advertising and survey partners before participating.

5. Data Security

We use secure cloud infrastructure and industry-standard security practices to help protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

While we work hard to protect your information, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Fraud Prevention

EarnFun actively monitors accounts for fraudulent activities, including fake referrals, multiple accounts, automated activity, reward abuse, and other violations of our Terms of Service.

7. Children's Privacy

EarnFun is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable laws in your country.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Updates become effective once published on this page.

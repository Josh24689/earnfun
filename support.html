Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn rewards?

You can earn rewards by completing surveys, offerwall tasks, watching rewarded advertisements, participating in promotions, and inviting friends through the referral program.

Why haven't I received my reward?

Some rewards require verification by our advertising or survey partners before they are credited. Processing times may vary depending on the provider.

How do withdrawals work?

Withdrawal requests are reviewed before processing to help prevent fraud and ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Only one EarnFun account is permitted per person unless expressly authorized by EarnFun.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Please email us with:

  • Your User ID (if available)
  • Your device model
  • Android version
  • Screenshots of the issue
  • A detailed description of what happened
